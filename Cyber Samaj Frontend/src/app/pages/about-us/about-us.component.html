About CyberSamajh

CyberSamajh is a community-driven initiative aimed at empowering citizens with digital safety awareness, reporting cybercrimes, and staying informed with real-time updates in the cyber world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the digital world safer and smarter for everyone. We provide tools, news, and awareness programs to help individuals understand, prevent, and report online crimes efficiently.

What We Do

  • Real-time reporting of online crimes
  • Cybersecurity news and education
  • Connecting citizens with law enforcement
  • Building digital awareness communities
